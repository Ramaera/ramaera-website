Agri business

Agri business is one of the most flourishing sectors of the industry and Ramaera Industries are planning to set forth its foot in this industry as well. We plan to play the role of the input providers, producers, processors as well as service providers in agribusiness. The ultimate motive is mainly to monitor transparency and supervised operations to provide qualitative product delivery at the customer end.

This agribusiness initiative by the Ramaera industry aims to solve two major issues in the supply chain system of agricultural business namely the over-chemical processing of fruits and vegetables poses a severe threat to the consumers and the second is providing a good resourceful percentage of revenue to the farmers owing to the unfair practices being laid on the farmers.

Chemical processing is laying severe risk to the health of children increasing the threat of cardiovascular diseases and cancer. There have been cases of an increase in breathing problems and lung diseases. Many ill practices are going on in the agricultural industry such as artificial ripening, adulteration, and also unfair pay practices. Ramaera Industries plans to eradicate these practices to provide the best to our consumers to make the nation immune and healthy.

Ramaera Industries Agribusiness is a safer alternative to the conventional farming method. Organic farming will utilise natural and harm-free methods completely avoiding the use of chemical fertilisers just for the greed of yield. Utilising safer technological alternatives, we aim to provide a resourceful yield that is free from the chemical hazards of farming.

There are numerous health benefits to consuming organic fruits and vegetables. Recent studies show that organic fruits and vegetables have a higher level of antioxidants as compared to the ones grown through conventional farming. Organic fruits and vegetables have a higher nutritional value as compared to the latter. Apart from this, we aim to achieve various sustainable goals through our Organic Agribusiness such as mitigating carbon levels in the atmosphere and managing soil erosion. Organic Agribusiness also aims to deliver nutritious and fresh fruits right to your homes, saving you time and providing you with health with ease. Order a variety of fruits and vegetables from our vast range with freshness and quality guaranteed.
